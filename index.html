Presentation-Friendly Explanation

• Expensive multiplayer games attract massive crowds but also receive stronger criticism due to bugs, toxic communities, and unrealistic expectations.

• Smaller indie games focus on polished gameplay and creativity, leading to higher satisfaction scores.

• Singleplayer games consistently maintain better emotional experiences because they avoid competitive frustration.

• The data suggests that popularity does not always equal quality.
